About Me

Hello! I'm Alexander Frost, a screenwriter and narrative designer with over 8 years of experience in crafting compelling stories for film, television, and digital media.

My journey began at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts, where I discovered my passion for exploring the human condition through visual storytelling. Since then, I've collaborated with studios and independent filmmakers alike, specializing in psychological thrillers and science fiction that challenge perceptions and provoke thought.

I believe that the best stories are those that strike a delicate balance between entertainment and substance â€” narratives that captivate audiences while simultaneously inviting them to reflect on the deeper aspects of our shared experience.

When I'm not crafting new worlds at my desk in Los Angeles, you can find me hiking the canyons or lost in the pages of speculative fiction, constantly seeking inspiration from both the natural world and the boundless realms of imagination.
